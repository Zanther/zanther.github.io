CONDEX PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: March 2026

ConDex ("the App") is developed by Steven Lattenhauer 2 ("the Developer").

DATA COLLECTION AND STORAGE
ConDex does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with the
Developer or any third parties. All data — including contacts, avatars,
photos, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's
Core Data framework.

ICLOUD SYNC
If you have iCloud enabled, ConDex syncs your data to your personal
iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is stored in
your private iCloud container and is accessible only to you. The
Developer cannot access, view, or retrieve any data in your iCloud
account.

CONTACTS
ConDex accesses your device's contacts (with your permission) to import
contact information into the app. This data never leaves your device or
your personal iCloud account.

LOCATION
ConDex uses your approximate location (city-level accuracy) solely to
display current weather information via Apple's WeatherKit service. Your
location is not stored, logged, or transmitted to any server controlled
by the Developer.

PHOTOS
When you select a photo for a contact, it is accessed via Apple's system
photo picker. The selected image is stored locally and in your iCloud
account. ConDex does not access your full photo library.

NOTIFICATIONS
ConDex may send local notifications for birthday reminders. These are
generated entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any
external server.

ANALYTICS AND ADVERTISING
ConDex does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising
frameworks. The Developer does not collect usage data of any kind.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
ConDex uses the following Apple first-party services: WeatherKit, MapKit,
CloudKit, and the Contacts framework. These services are governed by
Apple's own privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/privacy/.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
ConDex does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL
with a revised "Last updated" date.
